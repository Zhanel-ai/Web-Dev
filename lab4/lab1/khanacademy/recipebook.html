Recipe #1: Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2 people
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1

Steps:

  1. Mix all ingredients together.
  2. Heat a pan.
  3. Pour batter and cook until golden.

Source: AllRecipes

Recipe #2: Pasta

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 3 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 200 g
Tomato sauce 1 cup
Cheese 50 g

Steps:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water.
  2. Heat sauce in a pan.
  3. Mix pasta with sauce and add cheese.

Source: BBC Good Food
