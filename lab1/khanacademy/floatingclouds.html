What are clouds?

Why are some clouds yellow?

Yellowish clouds may occur in the late spring through early fall months during forest fire season. The yellow color is due to the presence of pollutants in the smoke.

In meteorology, a cloud is a visible mass of liquid droplets or frozen crystals made of water or various chemicals suspended in the atmosphere above the surface of a planetary body.

Terrestrial cloud formation is the result of air in Earth's atmosphere becoming saturated due to cooling of the air and adding water vapor.
